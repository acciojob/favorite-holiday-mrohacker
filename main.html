Goa


Goa is a popular holiday destination known for its beautiful beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich cultural heritage.

  1. Baga Beach – famous for nightlife
  2. Calangute Beach – largest beach in Goa
  3. Fort Aguada – historic fort with sea view
  4. Dudhsagar Falls – stunning waterfall
  5. Anjuna Market – popular flea market

  • Prawn Balchão
